1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies only for website analytics. These cookies help us understand how the Site is being used, measure the effectiveness of the content, and improve the user experience.

The only type of cookies we use are Analytical / Performance Cookies.

These cookies, provided by Google Analytics, allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is **aggregated and therefore anonymous**.
